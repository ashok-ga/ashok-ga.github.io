About Me

I'm a Lead Robotics Engineer currently working at General Autonomy. I hold a dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kanpur.

Previously, I worked at Testbook.com where I led server-side rendering improvements and built marketing automation tools like location-based targeting and deep-linking pipelines. I also interned at Siemens and Noccars where I worked on real-time dashboards, NREL’s tracking algorithm for solar panels, and IoT communications.

My academic work included building a brain-computer interface enabled autonomous wheelchair, using EEG, RTAB-Map, and EKF-based SLAM. I’ve also built a custom game engine from scratch during my time at IIT Kanpur.

I actively participate in robotics competitions and have led teams at ERA IITK. My core skills include Next.js, React, Node.js, Python, C++, AWS, and working with robotics tools like OpenCV, TensorFlow, and ROS.
